Genomics / Proteomics / Cell Biology / Integrative Analysis

Advancing CRISPR Therapeutics

Lead a project involving genetic editing for therapeutic applications. Collaborate with diverse teams to apply integrative analysis and multiple designs. Conduct molecular assays in vitro and in vivo to support project goals.

Genomics / Epigenomics / Cell Biology / NGS analysis

Novel Factors Specifying Chromatin Boundaries

Performed genomics analysis to track cohesin relocation without known insulators, identifying DNA motifs for various zinc finger proteins (ZNFs), including PATZ1. Conducted functional analyses revealing that PATZ1 acts as novel insulation factors critical for genomic boundaries and cellular fate.

Genomics / Proteomics / Phenotypic Screen

Unveiling ERF19: A Key Regulator of Plant Immunity

Conducted genetic screens to identify novel factors involved in plant defense against Botrytis cinerea, leading to the discovery of ERF19. Further research revealed that the NINJA co-repressor complex modulates the transcriptional activity of ERF19.

Genomics / Proteomics / Phenotypic Screen

Enhancing Crop Resistance Through Genetic Engineering

Leveraged fundamental plant science to engineer disease-resistant crops, specifically demonstrating broad-spectrum resistance in tobacco plants overexpressing Arabidopsis LecRK-VI.2. This protein boosts the innate immunity of transgenic plants, leading to enhanced resistance against 3 diverse bacterial pathogens.
